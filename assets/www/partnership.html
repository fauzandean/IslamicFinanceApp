Partnerships

Passive (Mudaraba) is a managed partnership between work (manager/entrepreneur) and capital(one or more investors). The components are capital(provided by the Rab al Mal), attracted by a clearly defined business plan to invest a lumpsum into the business.

Skills and Expertise is provided by the Mudarib or business manager.

Profits are shared according to ratio agreed, any losses are distributed in accordance with the proportion of capital provided Mudaraba contract is terminated anytime by either party

Joint Ventures (Musharaka) or Shirkah which means sharing and is same as Mudaraba except all partners provide capital and expertise. Profits are shared as agreed in the contract and losses are distributed in accordance with the proportion of capital provided.
