Agency

Wakala Agency contract is widely applied by banks and their clients. The concept ranges from brokerage services in permissible activities, to funds management (as an alternative to Mudaraba) and underwriting management in Islamic insurance, to serving as the agent (Wakil) for the buyer or seller on behalf of a contractor. In principal an agency contract is permissible with respect to all activities that are permitted to be discharged by the principle

Disclosed Agent is when an agent discloses his role to others. All documentation with the subject matter is done in name of principal, this is preferred

Undisclosed Agent is when an agent does not disclose, the suppliers cannot claim from bank but from agent. The agent has a fiduciary responsibility and hence acts as trustee in holding the assets of the principal or in executing the service.
