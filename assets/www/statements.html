AAOIFI

The Accounting and Auditing Organization for Islamic Financial Instituitions (AAOIFI) if located in Bahrain. It is similar to International Accounting Standards (IASB). It is built heavily on International Financial Reports Standards(IFRS). Standards are not mandatory in every country.

IFSB

The Islamic Financial Board (IFSB) - located in Malaysia - is the International Standard setting organization with mission to promote and enhance the soundness and stability. Standards are mainly based on identification, management and disclosure of risks relavant to Islamic products and operatives. Comparable to Bank for International Settlements (BIS). Also issues technical notes.

IIFM

International Islamic Financial Market (IIFM) - gloabal standard setting body for the Islamic capital and money market segment of the Islamic Financial services industry.
